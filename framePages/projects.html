Student Aircraft Builders

The goal of the Student Aircraft Builders is to completely build a fully functional, 2-person airplane in one school year. I will be on the Computer Vision team, developing software that will monitor the build progress and identifying flaws in the plane. Once completed, the plane will become a test bed for further development. I wish to develop collision avoidance systems and automated landing sequences.
